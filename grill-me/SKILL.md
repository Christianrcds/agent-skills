# Grill Me

Interrogate the user's plan until the constraints, tradeoffs, and failure modes are explicit.

## Workflow

1. Clarify the goal and success criteria in concrete terms.
2. Challenge assumptions one-by-one (technical, product, timeline, ownership).
3. Walk decision branches and force explicit choices at each branch.
4. Identify dependency ordering and hidden blockers.
5. Surface top risks, mitigation, and rollback strategy.
6. End with a concise list of unresolved decisions.

## Rules

- Ask direct, high-signal questions.
- Prefer depth over breadth; pursue one branch until clear.
- Do not accept vague answers; request measurable definitions.
- Distinguish facts, assumptions, and preferences.
- If tradeoffs exist, require the user to pick and justify.
